About AMN Group

“Strength in unity.”

AMN Group Inc was born from a simple observation: the construction and property management industries were fractured. For years, we operated as independent business owners, each mastering a specific trade—from roofing and electrical to interior design and plumbing.

We saw firsthand how clients struggled to coordinate between different companies, leading to delays, mismanaged schedules, and unnecessary costs. We unified our expertise into one streamlined operation.

Today, our team works as a single coordinated unit—delivering efficient timelines, consistent quality, and full accountability across every phase of your renovation. We do what others don't, offering in-house maintenance, renovations, landscaping, and snow removal for seamless, cost-effective service. No subcontractors, no hassle. Just expert care for your property. Whether you need full management or a one-time service, we're always one call away for commercial and residential needs.

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some of our frequently asked questions. Please click below to view answers.

1. What types of renovation services do you offer?

▼

We provide a comprehensive range of home renovation services, including kitchen and bathroom remodels, basement finishing, flooring installation, painting, and full-home renovations. Each project is customized to meet the specific needs and preferences of our clients.

2. Do you offer free estimates?

▼

Yes, we provide free, no-obligation estimates. We assess your project requirements and provide a detailed and transparent quote outlining scope, timeline, and costs.

3. How long will my renovation take?

▼

Project timelines vary depending on the scope and complexity of the work. Smaller projects may take a few days to a few weeks, while larger renovations can take several weeks or more. We provide a clear timeline before starting and keep you informed throughout the process.

4. Are you licensed and insured?

▼

Yes, our team is fully licensed and insured, ensuring that all work is completed safely, professionally, and in compliance with local regulations.

5. Do you handle permits and inspections?

▼

We can manage the permit and inspection process on your behalf, ensuring all work meets building codes and regulatory requirements.

6. What sets you apart from other contractors?

▼

At Amn Group Inc, we are your complete property partner, offering expert management and contracting services under one roof. Property owners can rely on us for comprehensive, hands-free management, while residential clients can hire us for any individual service. Our in-house team expertly handles everything from flat roofing, plumbing, HVAC, and electrical work to full renovations—including kitchen and bathroom makeovers with our interior design expertise. As well as painting, lawn care, and general maintenance. With Amn Group Inc, you get seamless quality and convenience, all from a single, trusted provider. Your property, perfected.

7. How do you ensure quality in your work?

▼

We use skilled professionals, high-quality materials, and proven construction practices. Each project is carefully managed and inspected to ensure it meets our standards and your expectations.

8. Can I make changes during the project?

▼

Yes, changes can be accommodated where possible. We communicate any impact on cost or timeline before proceeding, so you can make informed decisions.

9. How do payments work?

▼

Payment schedules are structured in stages, typically starting with a deposit followed by progress payments and a final payment upon completion. Full details are outlined in your project agreement.

10. Will I be able to live in my home during the renovation?

▼

This depends on the scope of the project. For smaller renovations, it is often possible to remain in your home. For larger projects, we may recommend temporary arrangements for comfort and safety.

11. What is the Tenant Relations service?

▼

Our Tenant Relations service ensures smooth communication and coordination between tenants and property managers during construction projects.

12. How do I get started?

▼

You can get started by contacting us to discuss your project. We will schedule a consultation, assess your needs, and provide a detailed estimate to move forward.
