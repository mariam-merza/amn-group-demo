Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 2026

AMN Group is committed to protecting your privacy and handling your personal information in a responsible and transparent manner. We are committed to excellence and security for every individual who visits our websites. We are required to request certain information from you in order to assess your situation and provide the most accurate answers possible to your questions. You can rest assured that your information will never be disclosed to any other site, business or individual unless you specifically request such information be disclosed. Please read below for any additional detailed questions you may have about our policy. Please do not hesitate to contact us with any questions or concerns.

Information We Collect

We may collect your name, email address, phone number, project details, and any information you voluntarily submit through our website or communication channels.

How We Collect Information

Information is collected when you contact us, request a quote, or interact with our website. Basic analytics data may also be collected automatically.

How We Use Information

We use your information to respond to inquiries, provide quotes, deliver renovation services, communicate project updates, and improve our website.

Cookies

Cookies are small text files that may be placed on your computer’s hard drive by our servers to identify your computer. We may use cookies to recognize you when you visit our sites, to personalize your experience and generally to serve you better. For example, if you enter your name, address, telephone number, email address, zip code or other personally identifiable information on one of our forms, such information may be, but is not necessarily always, stored in a cookie so you do not have to enter this information again. You may disable cookies in your browser settings. Instructions for disabling cookies are below.

How to Disable Cookies

You can control or disable cookies through your browser settings. Below are general steps for common browsers:

Google Chrome

Go to Settings → Privacy and security → Cookies and other site data. From here, you can block all cookies or third-party cookies.

Microsoft Edge

Go to Settings → Cookies and site permissions → Manage and delete cookies and site data. Adjust your cookie preferences accordingly.

Mozilla Firefox

Go to Settings → Privacy & Security → Cookies and Site Data. You can choose to block cookies or manage exceptions.

Safari

Go to Preferences → Privacy. Enable "Block all cookies" or manage website data settings.

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. Data may be shared with trusted subcontractors or service providers only when necessary to complete a project or comply with legal obligations.

Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information. However, no method of transmission over the internet is completely secure.

Your Rights

You may request access, correction, or deletion of your personal information by contacting us.
